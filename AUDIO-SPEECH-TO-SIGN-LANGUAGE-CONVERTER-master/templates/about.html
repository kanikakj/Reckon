About Sign Hub

In India, there are 63 million people, who have hearing impairments, according to the 2011 census. Among them, 76â€“89% of the hearing-impaired Indian population is illiterate in spoken, signed, or written languages. The absence of sign language interpreters and the lack of Indian Sign Language tools creates a communication gap between normal and hearing-impaired people. SignHub is a one stop platform where members of the hard of hearing community can engage in daily activities like socialising and can access information just like hearing individuals. It breaks the barrier of communication between normal and hearing-impaired people, making the interaction simpler and faster.

Meet the Team
